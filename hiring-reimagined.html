Async Interview Process

Record Your Interview

Create a 3-5 minute video introducing yourself and answering the following questions:

  • What excites you about this role?
  • What unique perspective would you bring to the team?
  • Share an example of a challenge you've overcome and what you learned

Submit Your Work

Along with your video, please provide:

  • Your portfolio or relevant work samples
  • Links to projects you're proud of
  • Any additional documents specific to the role

Next Steps

After review, qualified candidates will:

  • Receive feedback within 48 hours
  • Schedule a live conversation with the team
  • Discuss potential project collaboration
